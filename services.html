Gesha Mobile Banking

Gesha mobile banking is a service that allows our customers to transfer funds from the clients Gesha account to mobile money(Withdraw), from mobile phone to customers account(Deposit),
  • Mobile Phone Banking
  • Money Transfer
  • Registration
Gesha Mobile is a mobile banking service that allows Gesha customers to transfer funds between Gesha and mobile money, check their bank account balance & recent transactions, and pay for goods directly from their bank account as detailed below:

Transfer your funds between Gesha and mobile money

Dial *217*250# to move money between your Gesha account and Airtel or MTN Mobile Money account to deposit or withdraw respectively.

Deposit

Move funds from mobile money to your Gesha account Dail (*217*250#. for Airtel menu and *217*250#. for MTN menu)

Withdraw

Move funds from your Gesha account to mobile money Dail (*217*250#)

How do I register?

Fill an application form with a copy of your National ID. Submit your application,Get enrolled, you should receive an SMS on your phone which has a temporary pin that expires with in 24 hrs,register with that temporary pin. Change the temporary pin to your desired pin of your choice.

*217*250# to start transacting.
