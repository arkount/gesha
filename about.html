Gesha Microfinace

Is a microfinance institution registered in Uganda, It was incorporated under the Co’s Act &the Money Lenders’ Act. GESHA provides specially designed range of lending services to Ugandans especially to those who do not have access to the mainstream commercial banking services.

Gesha Microfinace

GESHA has its Head Office located on , Nansana, Hoima Road, and currently operates seven (8 satellite branches in Nansana, Makindye, Bwaise, Kireka ans Jinja in central region and Mbarara, Ntungamo and Rukiga in western Uganda.
ABOUT GESHA

Mission, Vision & Strategic statements At Gesha Microfinace

Our Vision

“To be the most efficient and reliable financial partner to the economically active Ugandans”.

Mission Statement

“To promote financial inclusion, economic participation and social prosperity for Entrepreneurs in Uganda”.

Our Goals

"Our main goal is to increase the value of the Institution to all our stakeholders."

Core Values.

  • Commitment- to achieving the Company‘s mission, objectives and meeting the needs of our clients in the best way possible.
  • Honesty& Integrity -We uphold the highest ethical standards. We seek to create an environment that enhances trust and confidence amongst all stake holders
  • Respect- we recognize the importance of diversity and therefore treat our internal and external customers and partners with utmost respect.
  • Team work- We work as a team, knowing that together each one achieves more and make a valuable contribution to the success and sustainability of GML.
  • Professionalism- to be professional in our actions to our clients, partners and each other.

    • Our objectives to achieve the main goal are;

  • To maintain 100 % daily collections for all groups/ zero over due at branches.
  • To reduce Grow our clientele across the branch network.
  • To ensure every branch has atleast 23 groups of at least 10 members each.
  • To recover from the previous groups / clients in defaults
  • To ensure we provide good and satisfactory customer service to our clients.
  • To hold staff accountable
  • To ensure gesha is afraud free environment

    Namakula Prossy

    Gesha Client

    My Name is Namakula Prossy. I was born 30 years ago in Isingiro District. I conduct my business in the Central Market, Mbarara and deal in fruits and fresh vegetables. I heard about Gesha Microfinance in 2017 through a friend who had an account with them. She advised me to do the same, and consequently, I opened the account and started saving. I started by saving Ugx 8,000 per week within the group, and after six weeks, I obtained a loan of Ugx 250,000 which I invested in the business. After paying it up fully, I obtained another loan of Ugx 400,000, and slowly advanced to bigger loans.
